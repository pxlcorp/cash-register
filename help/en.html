Introduction

Pxl Cash Register allows you to sell items quickly and easily.

First step: Connect a scanner

  • Get a hand scanner
  • Go to the menu Options
  • Go to the tab Scanner
  • Press the button Add scanner
  • Take the hand scanner
  • Press the left click while looking at the machine
  • Add a fixed scanner (Optional)
    • Get a fixed scanner
    • Take the hand scanner
    • Press the left click while looking at the fixed scanner

Second step: Register items

  • Find an item to sell
  • Take the hand scanner
  • Press left click while looking at the article
  • Change the information in the article
    • Go to the menu Items list
    • Find your item in the list (class: the model name)
    • Press the edit button (the blue button)
    • Enter the name and price
    • Press Confirm

Third step: To sell

  • Scan your item
    • Take the hand scanner
    • Press left click while looking at the article
    • Or move the item at the front of the fixed scanner
  • Add a service (Optional)
    • Press Add service
    • Enter the service description and price
    • Press Confirm
  • Wait until the customer completes the transaction
    • He must press use while looking at the machine (not on the screen)
    • He must select the payment method
    • He must then confirm
  • Print the receipt for the customer (Optional)

Fourth step: Money management

  • Go to the menu Money
  • To take Cash:
    • Press Take money
    • Enter the desired amount
  • Distribute Credit
    • Select the player that you want by pressing the button to the right of the name
    • Select the transfer method
    • Enter the desired amount
  • Deposit money
    • Press Deposit
    • Select the deposit method
    • Enter the desired amount

Fifth step: To connect cash registers

  • Go to the menu Options
  • Go to the tab Server
  • To host a server:
    • Press the button to the right of the password
    • Enter a password
  • To join a server:
    • Press Connect to another server
    • Enter the name and password of the server

Options

  • Access management:
    • Manage people authorized to use the machine.
    • The list of authorized people is shared to every machine connected to the same server.
  • Server options:
    • The server name is used as an identifier for the machines trying to connect to this one.
    • When there is no password, the server cannot be joined by a new machine.
    • To login, enter the name and password of the server in question.
  • Scanner management:
    • Save or remove scanners.
  • Permissions:
    • Only the owner can change permissions.
    • Allow to block access for employees.
